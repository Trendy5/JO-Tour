Our Vision

    JO TOUR is a local project created and deployed by a passionate group of pure Jordanians. We, as JO TOUR team, strive to showcase the glorious side of Jordan in order to enhance the Tourism Sector in our country. We believe that every single individual has the right to experience the beauty of this place and shall get a chance to spend a great time with their beloved ones in their favourite place. With that being said, JO TOUR presents the visitor with realistic information about different locations around Jordan. JO TOUR assures the delivery of actual footage taken by visitors. We also provide you with real prices and real comments to draw a full picture for you.

Our Mission

    According to the vision JO TOUR team has developed along the course of the project, we have taken the responsibility of providing the best experience in Jordan. We guide the steps of Jordanians and foreigners around the streets of Jordan to see the beautiful places, eat the delicious food and try amazing dishes. We also provide an extra service for the visitors to help them plan their trip from A to Z. We are willing to get the best prices for you, so that you would not have to worry about the money. It is all about the experience!
